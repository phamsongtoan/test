Vision

Leading agrochemical producer with high qualify, safe products and competitive prices, bringing a lots benefits to farmers, helping farmers increase their productivity with a bumper crop.

Mission

Becoming a leading agrochemical manufacturer in the world with advanced technology. Finding out and studying useful, effective solutions for farmers to improve their crops and productivity through new patents.

Commitment

We are committed to fulfilling our role as a leading agrochemical supplier and making our contribution to the development of Vietnam agriculture with high responsibility and the motto “Map Pacific – Helping farmers”.

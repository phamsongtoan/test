For use in
High selectively in efficacy, absolutely
Control for all weeds on rice farm: sedge, broad leaf weeds, Echinochloa crus galli etc.
Ingredients
Quinclorac 230%
Ethoxysulfuron 23%
Contact for detail
Direction for use
TARGET PETS
Sedges and broadleaf weeds
TIME TO APPLY
1-7 days after sowing
(Weed has 1-3 leaves)
DOSAGE
2kg/ha
(100gram/ 16 liters of water)
Note
TARGET PETS
Sedges and broadleaf weeds
Safety Precaution:
  • Formulation operator should wear rubber gloves, rubber boots, overalls and eye protection.
  • Do not smoke, drink or eat while using this product
  • Wash hand and exposed skin before meals and after work.
  • Do not re-use empty containers, destroy by cutting or crushing and bury them away from habition.
  • Store medications in a cool place, away from food sources and reach of children.
Aid:
  • If non-adhesive skin: Wash thoroughly with soap and water.
  • If sticky eyes: hold eye open and rinse under running water for 10-15 minutes.
  • If swallowed: give victims to health facilities and accompanying labels.
